# HR Analytics Dashboard



## Project Overview

This project analyzes employee attrition data using Excel, MySQL, Python, and Power BI to identify workforce trends and key factors influencing employee turnover.

## Tools Used

- Excel
- MySQL
- Python (Pandas & NumPy)
- Power BI
- DAX

## Dataset

Source: IBM HR Analytics Employee Attrition Dataset

The dataset contains employee information including:

- Age
- Gender
- Department
- Job Role
- Education Field
- Monthly Income
- Overtime Status
- Job Satisfaction
- Attrition

- HR_Analytics_Dashboard.pbix (Power BI Dashboard)

## Key Insights

- Overall attrition rate is 16.09%.
- Research & Development has the highest attrition.
- Employees aged 26–35 contribute the most to attrition.
- Laboratory Technicians have the highest attrition among job roles.
- Employees working overtime show higher attrition.
- Lower salary bands experience greater employee turnover.
- Job satisfaction appears to influence employee retention.
