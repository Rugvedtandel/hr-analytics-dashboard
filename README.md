## Project Overview

This project analyzes employee attrition data using Excel, MySQL, Python (Pandas & NumPy), and Power BI to identify workforce trends and factors affecting employee turnover.

## Dashboard KPIs

- Total Employees: 1,473
- Attrition Count: 237
- Attrition Rate: 16.09%
- Average Age: 37
- Average Salary: ₹6.5K
- Average Years at Company: 7

- sql_queries.sql (MySQL Queries)
- Dashboard_Screenshot.png (Dashboard Preview)
- HR_Analytics_Cleaned.csv (Dataset)

  ## Key Insights

- Overall attrition rate is 16.09%.
- Employees aged 26–35 show the highest attrition.
- Research & Development department has the highest attrition count.
- Employees working overtime are more likely to leave.
- Life Sciences and Medical education backgrounds contribute most to attrition.
- Lower salary bands experience higher attrition.
